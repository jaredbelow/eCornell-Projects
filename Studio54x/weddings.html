Weddings

Services > Weddings

When it comes to weddings, like other new beginnings, there is nothing more important than having a skilled photographer to capture each precious moment to be later retold.

We know that planning weddings can be stressful and costly, but don't let finding a quality photographer be another source of stress. We offer several different packages at all different price points. With all of our packages, we guarantee quality photos without breaking the bank.

A typical wedding package resembles the Madison and Eli wedding:

  • Over 120 guests
  • Custom "shot list" of 25+ edited proofs
  • 20+ edited proofs of the ceremony, from processional to recessional
  • 15+ shots of the couple during the reception
  • 100+ shots of guests during the reception

This package typically begins at $2,700 but will vary depending on the number of locations, travel, the types of poses, etc. A more accurate estimate is available with a consultation.
Venues with whom we have worked include:

  • The Grand Ithaca Hotel
  • Cayuga Pier
  • The Finger Lakes Barn
  • The State Hotel

To inquire about availability, special requests, and/or a detailed estimate, please request a consultation.
