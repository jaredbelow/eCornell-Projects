Welcome to the Ithaca Zoo

We are a non-profit, home to over 200 animals. We are committed to promoting the advancement of animal care and conservation. As you explore the different habitats and animal species, we hope you will learn more about our mission and join us in goal of ending animal extinction and helping the Earth.

Visitor Info

We are located at 123 Imagination Street in Ithaca, NY. We are open every day of the year, including holidays, from 8AM - 8PM. We have a free parking lot located at the front of the park. Please call ahead if you have a bus or oversized vehicle (vans not including mini-vans, trailers, etc.)
